Technology with a Gentle Touch

Leap Sign is working towards providing individuals who cannot speak

with the means to communicate in an efficient, cost-effective manner.

Get Your Point Across
Let's Talk

Conventional use of the American Sign Language has been too inefficient and is dependent on the other individual being fluent in ASL.

The Leap Sign's innovative user-friendly interface allows anybody to understand a Leap Sign user with relative ease.

Leap Motion Innovation
through Leap Motion

Through Leap Motion, those who speak through ASL will have access to an entirely new voice.

With the combination of a personalized user-interface and personal assistant, the Leap Sign molds itself an extremely effective communication tool.

Integration of
the Firebase database API

Firebase database interface is utilized when key words and phrases are recognized to allow for effective translation from ASL to automated voice.
